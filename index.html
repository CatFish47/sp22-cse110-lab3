The design of the project

The plan for the design of the project to make a tarot reading session. Person A will use their artistic talent to draw all tarot cards by hand as well as the asset for the back of the tarot cards. We will have a nice UI with buttons that will let the user draw however many cards that they want, which will reveal the backside of a tarot card. To flip the card, the user can click on the back of the card, and it will randomly reveal a card in either its reversed or upright form. Hovering over an unflipped card will highlight it to indicate that it is interactable.

What the planned workflow will be like

While Person A draws the assets for the tarot cards, replacement assets will be used to build the app. Person B will draw up a wireframe design for the app, and Person C, D, and E will code it. Meanwhile Person F will be implementing unit testing to ensure that every part of the app works as intended.
